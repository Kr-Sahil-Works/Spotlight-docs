Privacy & Data

Delete Your Spotlight Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Spotlight account and all associated data, you may submit a deletion request by contacting our support team. Once your request has been verified, your account and associated information will be permanently removed.

spotlight.support44@gmail.com

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email containing the following information:

  • Your Spotlight username
  • The email address used to register your account
  • Subject: Delete My Spotlight Account

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account information
  • Profile details
  • Posts and uploaded media
  • Stories
  • Notes
  • Chat messages
  • Followers and Following data
  • Bookmarks
  • Saved preferences

Data Retention

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days after verification. Some information may be retained for a limited period if required by applicable laws, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or security purposes.

If you have questions regarding your account or privacy, please contact our support team.
