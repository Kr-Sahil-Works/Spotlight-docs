Spotlight Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Spotlight account and associated data, email:

spotlight.support44@gmail.com

Request Format

  • Username
  • Email used for Spotlight
  • Subject: Delete My Spotlight Account

Data Deleted

  • Account information
  • Profile information
  • Posts
  • Stories
  • Notes
  • Messages
  • Followers and following data

Retention Period

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
