Child Protection

Keeping Spotlight Safe

Spotlight is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for everyone. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual exploitation, grooming, trafficking, or any form of abuse involving minors. Any violation of these standards may result in immediate removal of content, permanent account suspension, and reporting to relevant authorities when required.

Prohibited Content

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation involving minors
  • Grooming or attempting to contact minors for exploitation
  • Requests for CSAM or abusive material
  • Trafficking, coercion, or exploitation of children
  • Any illegal content involving minors

User Reporting

Spotlight allows users to report inappropriate content, accounts, messages, and other activity directly within the application. All reports are reviewed and investigated as quickly as possible.

Enforcement actions may include:
  • Immediate content removal
  • Temporary or permanent account suspension
  • Reporting to appropriate law enforcement authorities when legally required

Our Commitment

We continuously improve our moderation systems and community safety measures to help prevent abuse and protect our users. Spotlight complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with lawful investigations where required.
